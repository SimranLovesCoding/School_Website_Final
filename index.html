FACILITIES

Comptuer Lab

Computer Lab

The Computer Lab in our school is equipped with computing facility with Dual Core computers connected through the Local Area Network. This lab features 65 personal-computing workstations with Microsoft Windows 7 and Office 2007 application, with printer and scanner facility. Microsoft Windows software is available for word processing, statistics, spreadsheets and database management. Students use computers in their day-to-day study in order to meet the challenges of modern learning and to keep pace with global scenario. The lab is also equipped with a new 3D Printing Technology and has a latest 3D printer that allows students to print 3D objects.

Science / Chemistry Lab

Science / Chemistry Lab

The school has well-equipped laboratories for imparting practical knowledge in Physics, Chemistry and Biology. There are separate laboratories for each of the science subjects so that there is adequate equipment for every student to perform the experiments as per the ICSE guidelines. All the laboratories are well-stocked with sufficient chemicals, specimens and instruments so that every student gets the opportunity to carry out the experiments individually. Well-qualified and experienced teachers guide the students to develop practical skills and help abet student’s theoretical understanding.

Language Lab

Language Lab

The school has a Language lab on the first floor which helps students to learn languages in an entertaining and interactive way and enables students to acquire the 4 main language skills namely listening, speaking, reading and writing. Language lab allows students to reinforce material learned in class by putting them into practice through interactive activities.

E-Library

E-Library

E-library at Villa Theresa High School provides traditional materials as well as electronic resources. The carefully designed physical and digital spaces allow for various teaching styles to cater for diverse student research and learning. The E-Library contains a mixture of print materials such as books, magazines and journals, and electronic resources such as e-books, other web resources which are accessed by the students via a computer.
